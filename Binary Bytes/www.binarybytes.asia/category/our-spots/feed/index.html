This 6 months after stepping down as its CEO. He bought Basketball team LA Clippers and would be very much involved with them.
He is still Microsoft’s largest share holder, even more than the founder Bill Gates, who has been regularly selling his shares.

 

3. Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson will be in DC’s Shazam movie

After giving sometimes cryptic and sometimes very discrete suggestions for months, The Rock has finally said that he definitely is in a DC’s Shazam Superhero movie. Though yet not have the decision been finalised if he will be Shazam or his arch nemesis Black Adam.

We are his fan and can confidently suggest that he can and should play both.
