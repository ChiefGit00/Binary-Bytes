That is what Google is doing with its annual pwnium conference. Google in return will reward with million dollars to researchers.

As of now Google is making it a year round event instead of an annual affair.

What does this mean?
Requiring researches to submit the bugs in March and waiting for the conference, and hoping that everything goes well instead Google started VRP a Google Chrome Vulnerability Reward Program where when researchers come to bug exploit they will submit the bug and immediately become eligible for cash payout.

Google’s reward for now is “$∞ million,”that means if the bugs are disclosed and patched researchers can keep up the good work for Google and keep adding money to their account.

Google will also qualify the bugs in it’s own developed apps whether if found it iTunes or any other store.

Find more about this at  www.google.co.in/about/appsecurity/reward-program
