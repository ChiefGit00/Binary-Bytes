Thus in reality they practice with laptop and a wire strapped to a VR device on their head.

The Virtual Reality device simulates the movements similar to those in outer space. This helps to get used to and understand the controls of the jet packs.

By the way the jet packs used for emergency manoeuvring during space walks are called Simplified Aid for EVA Rescue or SAFER. These jet packs are completely normal and different from the  Manned Maneuvering Unit (the one that  Mr Clooney used in the movie Gravity).

Video of SAFER being attached | NASA (via Wikipedia)

SAFERs are backpacks which can be used to travel in space in case you are lost amongst stars and moons :)

Nikola Tesla
(10 July 1856 – 7 January 1943)

Back in May, cartoonist The Oatmeal publicly reached out to Elon Musk and asked for help in his mission to build a museum dedicated to Nikola Tesla. Musk had named his electric car company after Tesla.
At 2 A.M. the next morning, Elon responded by tweet: “I would be happy to help”.

Two months later (on Nikola Tesla’s birthday)Musk called him up last week and pledged two things:

1) A Tesla supercharger would be built just outside of the museum, making the museum a part of his nationwide Tesla recharging network.
2) A million dollars for the development/construction of the museum.
