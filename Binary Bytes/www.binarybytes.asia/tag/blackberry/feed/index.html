This is BlackBerry trying to do Phoenix. Under the new CEO (John Chen) the Canadian company formally known as RIM, is doing things differently.

Now when all smartphones are becoming similar rectangles with all touch display on the front, the Passport has a three rows of trademark BlackBerry QWERTY keyboard and an additional context sensitive row above. With Passport and later Classic and previously with Z3 this is BlackBerry making its long late comback.

It will be officially launched today in an event.  We will update as more things are known.
