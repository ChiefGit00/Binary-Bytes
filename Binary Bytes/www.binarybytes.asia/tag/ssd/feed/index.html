SSD – Binary Bytes http://www.binarybytes.asia Digital Life Mon, 13 Jun 2016 14:01:33 +0000 en-US hourly 1 http://www.binarybytes.asia/images/2014/08/favicon-2.03-150x150.png SSD – Binary Bytes http://www.binarybytes.asia 32 32 The First 3 Tera Byte SSDs Are Here! http://www.binarybytes.asia/digital-news/hardware/2015/02/fixstars-launches-first-3tb-ssd/ http://www.binarybytes.asia/digital-news/hardware/2015/02/fixstars-launches-first-3tb-ssd/#respond Sat, 21 Feb 2015 13:56:28 +0000 http://www.binarybytes.asia/?p=2714 Fixstars launched the world’s first 2.5 inch SSDs with 3 TB capacity in their home nation Japan last year in November.

These new SSDs use a NAND controller which is proprietary to Fixstars and prevent spikes and performance deterioration and gives consistent performance.

The large capacities are beneficial for 4K video recording and editing, and encrypted storage for film.

” The SSD-3000M/1000M were released in Japan last November, and have been getting great feedbacks from our customers,”
–Satoshi Miki, CEO & Co-Founder of Fixstars Corporation.

The reason behind these SSD being larger in storage and smaller in size is that they have self designed flash which are used in small scale mobile devices which allow them to fit in small space.

The drives gives speeds of 520MB/s for reading and up to 500MB/s for writing.

These SSD’s are based on 19nmMLC NAND and support TRIM, Advanced garbage collection and SMART.

The 1 TB and 3 TB variants of the drive are now available in North America.

Angelbird is a premium SSD manufacturer from Austria founded three years ago in 2011. They have a new line up of SSDS for Mac designated SSD wrk for Mac. 

They are the only third party SSDs that support TRIM for Macs. They come in variants of  128 GB ($99.99 or Rs. 6000), 256 GB ($159.99 or Rs. 9,800) and 512 GB ($299.99 or Rs. 18,00).

The SSDs are built keeping in the mind the quality of Apple’s products and consume low energy but still provide performance “achieved speeds of up to 563 MB/s while reading and 450 MB/s while writing.”

“The SSD is packed with custom NAND from Micron and powered by the SMI 2246EN “controller and connects with SATA 3.1 and is compatible with older Macs.

A warranty for 5 years is provided.
