These are live action shorts created by Canadian studio Playflight for Respawn Entertainment’s Titanfall. The series is directed by William Chang.

E3 2014 Clip

Director William Chang follows up his directorial debut of Titanfall: Free The Frontier with the second installment, blending high octane action with lightning paced visual effects. The Playfight team is dedicated to bringing faithful and exhilarating adaptation to Titanfall; marrying high quality visual effects, game IP and live-action content into one jaw dropping saga.
