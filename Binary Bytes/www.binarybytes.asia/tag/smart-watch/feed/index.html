Lets reveal it.

Apple introduced the Apple Watch at their event on 9 September 2014. The watch looks really beautiful on the wrist and even in the use so lets get started to know what they innovated.

1) Digital Crown : We all use watches and there is one thing common in every watch and that’s a crown. Apple used it the way no other did. They made it as the interface for the whole watch, to get it into the watch’s menu from any screen. If you are on the health app you can simply press the digital crown to get into the corner.

The main motive behind making the digital crown was to deliver the content without occupying the display with gestures. Pinch to zoom for maps will hide the content so instead you can use the Digital Crown and scroll to zoom in and zoom out and you can use it in the customize the watch, zooming in photos and much more more. Have a look at the digital crown.

2) The Tap and the Touch : Apple Watch understands the difference between a tap and a touch. It understand whether you are tapping the play button to play music or you are scrolling the ocean of beautiful apps at your home screen

3) Siri : The virtual assistant is  integrated within the Watch. You can command her to type a message, et reminders,  etc.

4) Smart Enough – The Watch itself is very smart. Whenever a message arrives for e.g “Which do you love the most GTX 750ti or 780?” the Watch automatically makes two options for reply – 750ti and 780. You can just tap on anyone of them to reply.

5) Watch Faces –  It is one of the best feature presented. Its like wallpaper for your watch. If its a butterfly it would change colours by closing and opening her wings, a cute Mickie as watch that uses the  two hands, making them second and minute pointers.

 

6) Health – Apple has boosted their focuses on health. By using sapphire sensors in the Watch it can calculate your daily exercise, how many steps you took and calories burned etc. Its all about health when its about Apple Watch.

7) Digital communication – You can do the digital communication with the others friends using apple watch by making the gestures such as smiley a sushi that you are hungry etc.

8) Flexible Retina – The newly designed  Photo Album will display massive pictures.

9) Apple Pay –  You can pay your amounts at the counter by just touching the watch at it.

 

What’s for the developers’s?

Apple has introduced WatchKit, where the developers will showcasing the talent over the watch.

The Watch uses Taptic Engine to capture your motion and display them on the iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus and uses S1 engine which is deeply complicated to understand at the moment.

Apple is releasing the Watch in three categories:

Apple Watch

The bands make the watch even more beautiful; made from custom engineered stainless steel,durable high performance and high top fine grain leathers.

Apple Watch Sport 
This specie of watch is made from lightweight anodized aluminum in silver and space gray.

Apple Watch Edition 

There are six different variants for made of hardened 18-Karat Gold.
