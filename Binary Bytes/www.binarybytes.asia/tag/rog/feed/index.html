This Republic of Gamers mouse from ASUS has a 6,400 dpi optical sensor which provides 50 g acceleration and 200 ips command rate with only 1.5mm of lift-off distance. The made-in-Japan Omron switches are rated at 20 million actions and can be easily swapped. Two additional switches are in the packaging.

The ROG logo on the mouse glows with red colour. The 2 meters long USB cable is detachable, the feets are Teflon coated and the body itself has rubber like coating. It does not have any weight customization option and weighs a static 116 g.

Dimensions : 126 mm x 67 mm 45 mm
