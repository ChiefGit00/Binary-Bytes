Electronic Sports League (ESL) One is one of the largest international championship for Counter-Strike, Dota 2 and Battlefield 4.

This year they are returning to Katowice, Poland for the biggest Counter Strike: Global Offensive tournament. 16 international teams will compete for a price pool of US$ 250,000 (₹ 1.5 cr). The finals will be held from 12 March to 15 March on the main stage in Spodek Arena in front of over 12,500 spectators.

Last year in Cologne, Germany it was Ninjas in Pyjamas who took home the $100,000 (₹ 60,00,000) prize with Fnatic coming second (read more about ESL One 2014 here).

Just like last year the top 8 teams from previous tournament will return as Legends and through combination of regional online and offline qualifying tournaments 8 new teams will be selected as Challengers.

ESL One 2015 Qualifiers

Eight teams have been directly invited for the offline qualifiers in Poland while 8 more teams will be selected through many online tournaments. Out of these 16 teams, 8 teams will go as cin the main tournament.

The 8 teams directly invited are :

  • Cloud9
  • Epsilon eSports
  • ESC Gaming
  • Team Dignitas
  • Team Liquid
  • Titan
  • Vox Eminor

Unlike last year the final playoffs for Challengers will happen live on-stage on 14 and 15 February.
