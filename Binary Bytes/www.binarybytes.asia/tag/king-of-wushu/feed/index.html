Snail Games (Suzhou Snail Electronic Co. Ltd.) who launched Age of Wushu (or Age of Wulin, Age Of Kung Fu), a martial arts-themed MMORPG last year have developed and MMO MOBA or Multiplayer Online Battle Arena game called King of Wushu.

It uses CryTek’s CryEngine 3 to create full 3D environment.

After passing away of a great emperor, martial arts champions have gathered from all across the land to demonstrate their power and skills, and the arena is their means of becoming the King of Wushu.

The 5-versus-5 matches in the game have the objective to destroy the enemy base while defending your own. You will have a wide array of martial arts-inspired abilities at your disposal and an action combat system that includes dodges, jumping, and attack combos.

The game is in early development and thus the release date is yet to be announced.

Developers : Snail Games
