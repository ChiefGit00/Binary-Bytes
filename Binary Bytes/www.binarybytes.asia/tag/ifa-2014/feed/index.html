It has not been more than six months since Sony’s  flagship had been released. Still Sony has come up with something better for their customers. Here is Sony new flagship phone the Z3.

Have a look at the specs :

SoC Snapdragon 801
CPU 2.5 GHz quad-core
GPU Adreno 330
RAM 3 GB
Storage 16/32 GB
OS Android 4.4.4 (KitKat)
Display 5.2-inch, 1080p
Camera 20.7 MP (back) + 2.2 MP (front)
Durability Water and dust proof(IP65 and IP68)
Colours white, black, silver, green and copper
Battery Capacity 3,100 mAh
Dimensions 146.5 x 72 x 7.3 mm

Updates in Z3:

1) A higher waterproofing scale.

2) The 20.7  MP camera is said to have a low light performance  upper limit of ISO setting of 12,800. No smartphone has did that yet. Sony’s Intelligent Active mode offers an improved digital stabilization and operates on the Steadyshot which makes it high end devices of the Sony.

3) In the Audio department it provides high scaling audio resolution playback with upscaling engine called DSEE HX which enhances the audio from lower-resolution.

4) Remote Play  One of the biggest feature brought into the phone. Users can enjoy their PlayStation 4 games right into their mobile when connected to preferably the same WiFi network. It comes for other Z3 family members i.e Z3 Compact, Z3 Tablet Compact.

Samsung with some of the above features has made many other updates refinements  in their Note 4. We start from :

1) S Pen : Note 3 had a limitation in S Pen to be only worked until and unless you get it on the screen.While in the note 4 Samsung has got it corrected and let users to use the s pen functionality from any angle by doubling the pressure sensitivity from the Galaxy Note 3.

2)Say Cheese  : we have been watching this feature since S3 that users are allowed to take selfies or photos by just saying ‘cheese’. Previously it really didn’t worked quite well but as demonstrated it has been improved and really works now.

3) Samsung VR : yeah this what we all were waiting for the Samsung to come out with the galaxy virtual headset where you can just put out the screen with resolution of (2560×1440) Super AMOLED display and enjoy the games,videos at their by getting it into that world just like Oculus Rift.

5) Better Camera : Samsung has claimed that the front camera will be capturing 60% more light than previous and to get better selfies. Samsung improved front camera angles by a default 90-degree shooting angle and up to 120 degree wide angle to help users capture better selfies.

6) Fast charging  : From 0 to 100 in 99 minutes. This would also be improved in android L due to project Volta
