The popular manga series started serialization in Weekly Shonen Jump in 1999, so its been 15 years since. Written by Masashi Kishimoto, Naruto follows the adventures of the namesake Ninja on his way to become Hokage. Two main Anime series and several movies and games have been set in this world of Shinobi.
