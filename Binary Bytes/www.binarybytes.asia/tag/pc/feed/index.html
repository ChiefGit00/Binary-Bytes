After months of wait, PC drivers for XBox One controllers are available for download.

XBox 360 controller has been the most popular game controller for PC for many years, mostly because of its very good support, influx of not to good ports (hence requiring a controller instead of mouse/keyboard) and also due to its excellent design.

 

With XBox One last year Microsoft released a new refined controller with “40 technology innovations“. Most importantly it has a new D-pad, thumbsticks and triggers.
