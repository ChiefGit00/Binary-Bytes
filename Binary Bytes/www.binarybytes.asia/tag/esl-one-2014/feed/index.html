ESL One is a competitive professional Counter Strike: Global Offensive tournament held in Europe. It is its second year.

Electronic Sports League is an international championship for Counter-Strike, Quake Live, Starcraft 2 and League of Legends.

16 teams will fight in CS:GO for the prize pool of $250,000 (Rs. 1.5 cr) at Cologne, Germany during GamesCom in the second ever world CS:GO championship.

Teams

Legends are the Top8 teams from last years ESL One Katowice, Poland and got direct entry in the tournament. 

Challengers had to qualify through various regional qualifiers.

Groups


You can view the live Telecast on Twitch.

Prize money :

1st $100,000 (Rs. 60,00,000)
2nd $50,000 (Rs. 30,00,000)
3/4 $22,000 (Rs. 13,00,000)
5-8 $10,000 (Rs. 6,00,000)
9-16 $2,000 (Rs. 1,20,000)

Quarter Finals Playoffs

Two teams per group went to the quarter finals and from these 8 teams will emerge the 4 teams going to Semi-finals.

The ESO One quarter-finals will begin at 09:00 CEST (12:30 IST) on 16 August 2014.
