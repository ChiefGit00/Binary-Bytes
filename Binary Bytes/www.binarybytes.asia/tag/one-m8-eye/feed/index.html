Now the two devices along with an updated M8 have been launched in India.

The highlight feature of the Eye line-up is a 13 MP camera. It debut on Desire Eye which has the same spec camera in the front and back, targeted to the selfie crowd.

HTC M8 Eye

 SoC Qualcomm Snapdragon 801
 CPU 2.5GHz quad-core
 CPU Adreno 330
RAM 2 GB

OS Android 4.4.4 (KitKat)

Camera 13 MP + dual LED flash (front) and 5 MP (front)
Colours Gunmetal Gray, Glacial Silver, Amber Gold
Battery Capacity 2600 mAh (non-removable Li-Ion)
Dimensions 146.4 x 70.6 x 9.4 mm
Weight 160 grams

It is all in all the same phone as the original HTC M8 with only the 4 Ultra Pixel back camera replaced by 13 MP sensor. It still retains the dual depth sensing camera from the original, used to collect depth data to add post picture focus manipulation. Plus the best in class BoomSound front stereo speakers.

HTC M8 Eye is a high end phone is priced at Rs 38,990 (US $649) and will only be launched in India and China.

HTC Re

Along with these two HTC also launched the one of its kind camera called Re. It is a small form factor 16 MP camera without a viewfinder so that we many spend our time absorbing the event in real time with our eyes instead of a digital display.

It is said to exclusive to Snapdeal till stock runs out and costs Rs. Rs. 9,990 ($150), though we were yet not able to find any listing.
