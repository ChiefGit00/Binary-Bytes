Lenovo is all set to re-dive in the tablet market with another Yoga launch and this time with many more features.

 CPU Intel Atom Z3745 Quad core @ 1.33 GHz (1.86 GHz)
RAM 2 GB
Storage 32 GB + microSD slot (up to 64 GB)
OS Android 4.4 KitKat
Display 13.3″ (2560 x 1440) IPS
Camera Rear: 8MP f2.2 Auto-focus (back) + Front: 1.6MP HD
Battery Capacity 9600 mAh (Li-Ion)

The Yoga line-up of laptops and tablets from Lenovo have been, since they were launched many years back, about comfortable contraption. Factor over form.

The Yoga Android tablets always had the recognisable roll/rod at one side. It was designed to provide grip of one hand portrait view. This time the roll/rod (what to call it?) is much more uniquely useful. It houses a powerful (for the size) projector and combined with the JBL speakers can act as a quick and dirty cinema.

The tablet is priced at $499.99 (Rs. 30,000) and is up for purchase.


The stand on the Yoga Tablet 2 Pro also can be pulled all the way to 180 degrees (vertical) and the cavity for the camera and speakers used to hang the tablet!

Aston Kutcher says that Yoga Tablet 2 Pro is primarily geared to be an entertainment device because Lenovo’s data shows that 80% of tablets are used at homes.
